About SKY DIAMOND LIMITED

Sky Diamond Limited, based in Accra, Ghana, specializes in gold and diamond trading, offering AU gold bars and rough gemstones. The company also facilitates investments in real estate, oil and gas, and factory projects with access to $6 billion in funding. Led by CEO Mr. Jacob Ameh, it connects clients with lucrative business opportunities.
